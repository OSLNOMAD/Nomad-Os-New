Role & Context
You are a senior product designer and front-end UI engineer redesigning an existing login page that feels cramped, dense, and visually suffocating. The goal is to create a calm, premium, modern SaaS login experience that feels trustworthy and breathable.

Core Problems to Fix

Elements are too close together vertically and horizontally

Typography hierarchy is weak

The form feels glued to the page instead of intentionally placed

No clear visual focus or breathing room

Left and right sections feel like they are competing instead of cooperating

Design Direction

Clean, modern SaaS aesthetic

Spacious layout with generous padding and margins

Clear visual hierarchy and alignment

Subtle depth and softness, not flat and not loud

Feels reliable, premium, and calm

Layout Instructions

Use a two-column layout on desktop:

Left column (40–45% width): brand and marketing

Right column (55–60% width): authentication form

Center the login form vertically within the right column

Place the form inside a soft card container with:

Rounded corners (12–16px)

Very subtle shadow or frosted-glass effect

Clear internal spacing between elements

Spacing Rules

Use large vertical spacing between sections (24–32px minimum)

Inputs should have comfortable height (48–56px)

Buttons should feel wide and intentional, not squeezed

Avoid stacking text too tightly, increase line-height across all copy

Typography

Clear hierarchy:

Logo

Page title (“Welcome Back”) as the main focus

Supporting subtitle below it

Use modern sans-serif typography

Increase line height for readability

Reduce visual noise by limiting font weights

Left Panel (Brand Section)

Full-height gradient background in Nomad green tones

Large brand headline with strong contrast

Short, readable supporting copy with ample spacing

Display key stats (24/7 Support, 99.9% Uptime, 50+ States) in a horizontal row with space between them

Keep this side visually calm, not overloaded

Right Panel (Login Form)

Card-based form with clear separation from background

Inputs with:

Rounded corners

Subtle borders

Soft focus states

“Sign In” button should be visually dominant and easy to tap

Secondary actions (Forgot password, Sign up) visually de-emphasized

Align everything cleanly to a single vertical grid

Visual Polish

Use subtle gradients, light shadows, and soft highlights

Avoid harsh borders or overly dark lines

Add whitespace intentionally everywhere

Nothing should feel cramped or rushed

Responsiveness

On mobile:

Stack sections vertically

Brand section collapses to a minimal header

Login card remains centered with padding from screen edges

Touch-friendly spacing throughout

Goal
The final design should feel:

Calm instead of crowded

Premium instead of cheap

Intentional instead of accidental

Something a serious SaaS company would proudly ship
